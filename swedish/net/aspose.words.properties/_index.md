| [CustomDocumentProperties](./customdocumentproperties/) | En samling anpassade dokumentegenskaper. |
| [DocumentProperty](./documentproperty/) | Representerar en anpassad eller inbyggd dokumentegenskap. |
| [DocumentPropertyCollection](./documentpropertycollection/) | Basklass för[`BuiltInDocumentProperties`](../aspose.words.properties/builtindocumentproperties/) och[`CustomDocumentProperties`](../aspose.words.properties/customdocumentproperties/) samlingar. |
