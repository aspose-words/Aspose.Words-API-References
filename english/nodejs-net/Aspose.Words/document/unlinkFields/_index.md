## unlinkFields() {#default}

Unlinks fields in the whole document.


```js
unlinkFields()
```

### Remarks

Replaces all the fields in the whole document with their most recent results.

To unlink fields in a specific part of the document use [Range.unlinkFields()](../../range/unlinkFields/#default).
