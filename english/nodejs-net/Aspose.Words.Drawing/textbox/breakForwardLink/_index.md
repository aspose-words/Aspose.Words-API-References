## breakForwardLink() {#default}

Breaks the link to the next [TextBox](../).



```js
breakForwardLink()
```

### Remarks

[TextBox.breakForwardLink()](./#default) doesn't break all other links in the current sequence of shapes.
For example: 1-2-3-4 sequence and [TextBox.breakForwardLink()](./#default) at the 2-nd textbox will create
two sequences 1-2, 3-4.
